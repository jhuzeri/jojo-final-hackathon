# Explain Like I'm Grandma 👵

Grandma, this small project is like a simple question‑and‑answer machine.

You type something on a webpage, and the computer thinks for a moment and sends a reply back.

The part you see on the screen is like a little form where you type your message.

The part you don’t see is a tiny helper inside the computer that reads your message and prepares an answer.

Both parts work together to show how websites talk to computers behind the scenes.

It’s just a small demo to show how information moves from you to the computer and back.
